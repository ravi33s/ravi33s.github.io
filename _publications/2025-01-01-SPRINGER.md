abstract: "The convergence of blockchain technology, the Internet of Things (IoT), and the metaverse presents transformative opportunities for creating secure, decen-tralized, and scalable infrastructures. This chapter explores how IoT serves as a foundational enabler for blockchain-enabled meta-verse systems, particularly in the context of smart wireless sensor networks (WSNs). IoT devices, ranging from edge sensors to industrial-grade equipment, generate vast amounts of real-time data that can be securely integrated into the metaverse using blockchainâ€™s decentralized ledger capabilities. We discuss the role of IoT platforms like Azure IoT Central, Azure Digital Twins, and IoT Edge in bridging the physical and digital worlds, enabling real-time synchronization, analytics, and autonomous control within the metaverse. Furthermore, we examine how blockchain enhances security, trust, and transparency in IoT-driven metaverse ecosystems by ensuring immutable data transactions and decentralized governance. The chapter also highlights emerging paradigms such as digital twins, artificial intelligence (AI)-driven insights, and sustainable architectures that leverage blockchain to optimize resource utilization and scalability. Finally, we address challenges such as cross-chain interoperability, energy-efficient consensus mechanisms, and regulatory considerations for deploying IoT in blockchain-enabled metaverse infrastructures. Through case studies and technical insights, this chapter provides a comprehensive roadmap for researchers and practitioners aiming to harness the synergy of IoT and blockchain in shaping the future of the metaverse."
